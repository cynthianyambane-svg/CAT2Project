About EduLearn

Our mission, team, and the story behind the platform

Who We Are

EduLearn is a Nairobi-based online learning platform founded in 2022 with the goal of making quality education accessible to every student across Africa. We partner with industry experts and universities to offer practical, career-focused courses.

Whether you're a high school student preparing for university or a professional looking to upskill, EduLearn provides structured learning paths that fit your schedule and goals.
