Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning with EduLearn

Getting Started

Click the Get Started button on our homepage, fill in your name, email address, and a password, then verify your email. Your account will be active immediately and you can enrol in any course right away.

Most of our courses are designed for complete beginners. Each course page clearly states the prerequisites — if none are listed, you can start with zero experience. We recommend starting with our free introductory modules to see if the pace suits you.

Yes. EduLearn is fully mobile-responsive. You can watch video lessons, complete quizzes, and track your progress from any smartphone or tablet. We also have a downloadable app available for Android devices.

Payments & Pricing

We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfer. For M-Pesa payments, you'll receive a prompt directly to your phone after checkout. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

Yes. For courses priced above KSh 5,000, you can split payment into two or three monthly instalments at no extra cost. Select the instalment option at checkout and follow the prompts to set it up.

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within 7 days of purchase and have completed less than 20% of the course, you can request a full refund by contacting our support team.

Certificates & Completion

Yes. Every learner who completes all lessons and passes the final assessment with a score of 70% or above receives a verifiable digital certificate. You can download it as a PDF and share it on LinkedIn or with employers.

Our certificates are increasingly recognised by tech companies and SMEs across East Africa. We are also working towards accreditation with TVETA (Technical and Vocational Education and Training Authority) in Kenya. Many of our graduates have successfully used their certificates in job applications.

You can retake the final assessment up to three times at no extra cost. If you don't pass after three attempts, you can contact our support team to review your progress and get personalised feedback before trying again.
