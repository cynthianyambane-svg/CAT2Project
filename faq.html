Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EduLearn

Courses & Learning

Browse our Courses page, select the course you want, and click the enrol button. You will be prompted to create an account or log in, then proceed to payment. Once payment is confirmed, you get instant access to all course materials.

Most EduLearn courses are self-paced, meaning you can study at any time that suits you. Some advanced courses include optional live sessions with the instructor on weekends. Check the individual course page for specific scheduling details.

Once enrolled, you have lifetime access to the course materials. This means you can revisit lectures, download resources, and review assignments at any time, even after completing the course.

It depends on the course level. All beginner-level courses assume no prior knowledge — just a computer and internet connection. Intermediate and advanced courses list their prerequisites clearly on the course page. We recommend reading those before enrolling.

Payments & Refunds

We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfers. For M-Pesa payments, use our Paybill number 400200 and your email address as the account number. You will receive a confirmation email within 5 minutes of payment.

Yes. For courses priced above KES 10,000, we offer a two-instalment plan — 50% upfront and the remaining 50% within 30 days. Contact us at info@edulearn.co.ke to set up an instalment agreement before enrolling.

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all courses. If you are not satisfied within the first 7 days of enrolment and have completed less than 20% of the course, you can request a full refund by emailing info@edulearn.co.ke with your order details.

Certificates & Results

Yes. Upon successful completion of all modules and the final assessment (minimum score of 70%), you will receive a digital certificate from EduLearn. You can download it as a PDF and share it directly to your LinkedIn profile.

Our certificates are recognised by a growing number of employers across Kenya and East Africa, particularly in the technology and digital marketing sectors. We are also working with university partners to offer credit recognition for selected courses.
